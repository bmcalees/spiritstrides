HELPING SMALL BUSINESSES SUCCEED.

We are a single point of connection to all of the Columbus region’s business resources.

Columbus boasts an amazing small business ecosystem. Our city has a strong economy, a diverse and educated workforce, capital resources, and an unparalleled network of entrepreneur support organizations for budding entrepreneurs and small business owners.

Our goal is to help you assess your challenges, guide you to the resources you need, and help you leverage them to plan, launch, and grow your business. The success of your business is a success for our region.

Not sure where to start? Let us help you. Email success@columbussbc.org or call 614-285-7736 today!
